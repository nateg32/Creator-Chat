# Code Of Conduct

Creator Chat should be a practical, respectful place to collaborate.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive.
- Assume good intent while still being clear about problems.
- Keep feedback focused on the work.
- Respect privacy and do not post secrets, private data, or sensitive logs.
- Be mindful that creator content, likeness, and voice raise real consent and attribution concerns.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Posting credentials, exploit details, or sensitive data in public issues.
- Encouraging misuse of scraped content or creator likeness.
- Spam or intentionally disruptive behavior.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit or remove comments, close issues, or block contributors when behavior harms the project or community.

Report concerns through the safest available project contact path. For security-sensitive matters, follow `SECURITY.md`.
